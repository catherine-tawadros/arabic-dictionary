DICTIONARY


Welcome to my Arabic dictionary!

This dictionary is specifically designed for the introductory Arabic sequence at Northwestern University, which teaches the Levantine dialect. It is developed based on in-class material and lessons from عربيّة الناس and is updated frequently in order to stay up to date on class materials.

Search by words or phrases (i.e. student, thank you), or by categories (i.e. countries, colors).

This project is still under development. Feel free to contact me with any questions and/or corrections.

Thank you for visiting!
